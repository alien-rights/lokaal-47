Mission

At Lokaal 47, we believe in making a difference. This commitment is deeply integrated into the fabric of our operations, shaping a space where every visitor is encouraged to think bigger and bolder. Lokaal 47 is more than a venue for meetings; it's a catalyst for creativity, designed to stimulate the senses, awaken the mind, and inspire meaningful decisions. We’re committed to this vision by not just dreaming big but also acting responsibly. We support local businesses, embrace sustainable practices, and give back to our community.

Supporting Local Businesses and Communities

Our community is our strength. We take pride in partnering with local businesses, providing them a platform to showcase their products and services. This not only boosts the local economy but also fosters a sense of community spirit. By sourcing locally, we reduce our environmental footprint and support the growth of our neighbors. Our initiatives include collaborating with nearby farms for fresh produce.

Commitment to Sustainability

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We are constantly innovating to reduce our environmental impact, incorporating practices that significantly lower our ecological footprint. By serving water in pitchers instead of single-use plastic bottles and offering leftover-bags to minimize food waste, we're taking concrete steps towards a more sustainable operation. We encourage our guests to adopt environmentally friendly travel options, like cycling or using public transport, aligning with our commitment to green practices. Furthermore, our energy needs are met through non-fossil sources, including solar panels, heat pumps, and efficient hot water boilers, reflecting our dedication to utilizing renewable energy.

Charitable Contributions

Giving back is a fundamental part of our philosophy. With every booking, we proudly donate 5% to one of the four charitable organizations we've partnered with, which can be found below. This commitment ensures that our success contributes to a wider good, supporting causes that range from environmental conservation to aiding underprivileged communities.

  • Rainforest Trust
  • NAACP LF
  • Heart of Dinner
