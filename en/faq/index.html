Is there parking?

In line with our commitment to sustainability, we encourage biking or public transport. For those driving, carpooling is appreciated. We offer 2 complimentary on-site parking spaces (also for loading and unloading). Nearby, 10 paid parking spots are available within a 5-minute walk, at a rate of €7.50 excluding VAT per car.

What technology can offer in-house (screen, sound, projection)?

Our venue is equipped with high-speed Wi-Fi and state-of-the-art interactive screens, perfect for both engaging presentations and seamless business calls.

How flexible is the space configuration for different event layouts?

Our space easily adapts to a variety of configurations to match your event's unique needs. Please share your preferences via email when booking, so we can tailor the setup just for you.

What guidelines are in place for guests at the venue?

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience for all our guests, we adhere to a specific set of guidelines outlined in our House Rules. Additionally, we comply with the AVG Privacy Law and the Uniforme Horeca Voorwaarden to maintain the highest standards of service and privacy.

Are there any outdoor facilities available for use?

Yes, we have a small covered outdoor area for breaks and a garden for enjoying lunch in good weather.
