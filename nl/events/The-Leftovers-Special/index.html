The Leftovers Special

4:00PM – 8:00PM

TUE, MAR 26TH

This Thanksgiving, leave no leftovers behind. Prep for Thanksgiving (and the days after) with Melissa Clark and Vaughn Vreeland at iconic sandwich shops.

Your Admission Includes

  • Cooking demonstration and tasting with Melissa Clark or Vaugh Vreeland.
  • Q. and A. session with Melissa Clark or Vaughn Vreeland.
  • Complimentary to-go Thanksgiving leftovers sandwich from your host restaurant.

Itinerary

  • 16:00 – Masa Memory cacao Champurrado welcome drink. Champurrado is a chocolate-based atole, a warm and thick Mexican beverage, made with masa harina.
  • 16:30 – Maiz soaking and grinding how to.
  • 17:15 – Tamal making workshop.
  • 18:15 – Farmer's market snack before a farm walk.
  • 18:30 – Farm walk to visit the avocado orchards, animals and fruit trees.
  • 19:15 – Return to collect tamales out of their cooking pots and make boxes to take home.
